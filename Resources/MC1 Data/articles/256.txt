TISKELE BEND EXPLOSION AND FIRE 

 

2000/08/17
 

ABILA, Kronos - A spark at the GAStech International-owned well at the Tiskele Bend site ignited methane gas several hundred feet underground, causing a fireball and fire that burned for about an hour is the findings from a dual GAStech - government investigation of the explosion and subsequent fire Sunday morning.
