PROTESTS SWELL IN ABILA IN RESPONSE TO CALLS TO ACTION 

 

2012/09/08
 

 

ABILA, Kronos - Thousands of people lined the roads on Thursday in a display of antigovernment solidarity.  Karel was the prior leader of the Protectors of Kronos before his premature death in 2009.  His death, thought to be a murder by many, prompted annul rallies to bring the message of the Protectors of Kronos to the public.
