Ministry of Health Investigates Contamination Complaints 

 

1998/11/10
 

ABILA, Kronos - Minister of Health Cesare Nespola announced today that his organization would be investigating claims of abnormal health issues in areas surrounding the Tiskele Bend drill site.  However, I believe it is important as Minister of Health to do due diligence and investigate the reports of sickness near the Elodis township.
