LANDSLIDE VICTORY FOR PRESIDENT KAPELOU

2 October 2000

ABILA, Kronos - The people of Kronos have overwhelmingly elected new President Dorel Kapelou II. Former President Luis Araullo conceded defeat yesterday when early returns came in strong for Kapelou II. Today marks a new dawn for the people of Kronos and a bright path forward for the country.

The results for the 1 October election are as follows: Dorel Kapelou II elected with 62% of the vote and Luis Araullo receiving just 36% of the vote. A resounding victory for the new elected President and a clear mandate for Kapelou's vision for the country.
