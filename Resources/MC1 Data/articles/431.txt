POK Kidnaps GAStech Employees 

 

2014/01/20
 

ABILA, Kronos - The Protectors of Kronos (POK) are suspected of kidnapping up to fourteen GAStech employees from their headquarters building earlier today.  One employee, Edvard Vann of Abila, was questioned for hours because he shares a family name with several known POK members.
