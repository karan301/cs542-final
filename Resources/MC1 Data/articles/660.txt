By Haneson Ngohebo
20 January 2014

1532 UPDATE - A GAStech - Kronos employee departing the headquarters building who declined to provide a name said that a couple of unknown people were in the back of the conference room where the GAStech meeting took place this morning. The unknown "shadowy" people lurking around the meeting room "weren't GAStech employees" and were "dressed in black and seemed to be standing, watching in the background."
