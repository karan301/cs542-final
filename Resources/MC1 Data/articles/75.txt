Kapelou Calls POK "A Gang of Criminals" 

 

2012/06/22
 

ABILA, Kronos - In a press release today, President Kapelou today called the POK "a gang of criminals, thugs, and malcontents who have become a pestilence to our peaceful way of life."  President Kapelou has called for increased police patrols of the Elodis area and has said that all future POK demonstrations will be "met with maximum police force."
