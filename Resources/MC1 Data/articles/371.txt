HANK FLUSS GASTECH COO DIES AT 73 

 

2003/05/17
 

CENTRUM, Tethys - Hank Fluss, COO of GAStech International, died of a heart attack at his desk in the company's downtown Centrum headquarters.  

 

Hank Fluss is survived by his wife of 54 years, Beatriz Fluss, his 5 children, and 12 grandchildren.
