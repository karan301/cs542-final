LEAF 2004 OF THE FACT OF THE PUBLIC HEALTH OF ELODIS



2004/05/31


ELODIS, Kronos - the center of the health of the community of Elodis has published an update to the health for all the study made in 1998.  The index of mortality and the incidence of the chronic disease increased on 252% as of previous years.  The report detailed soon will be fixed to the municipal Web site of Elodis.
