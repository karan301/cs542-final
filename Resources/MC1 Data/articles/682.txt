1235 UPDATE - Rumors abound in regards to the potentially missing GAStech executives. Some suggest that the newly vested executive group skipped the government reception to avoid the press and left to an unknown resort location to celebrate their recent windfall.
However, this rumor does not explain the police presence nor does it explain the fire department's search of GAStech headquarters.
