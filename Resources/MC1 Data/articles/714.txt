A CELEBRATION OF UNFETTERED KLEPTOCRACY 

 

2014/01/19
 

ABILA, Kronos - Following the annual GAStech-Kronos corporate meeting GAStech executives and several high-ranking Kronos government officials will attend a soiree at the Kronos capital building to celebrate the unfettered kleptocracy that has kept Kronos public officials in mansions and luxury vehicles.
